# Decision Quality Checks

Use these checks before finalizing an architecture option.

## Completeness Checks
- Is the decision question explicit and bounded.
- Are at least two viable options evaluated.
- Are criteria tied to real constraints.
- Are rejected options documented with specific reasons.

## Rigor Checks
- Is scoring evidence-based instead of preference-based.
- Are assumptions listed and testable.
- Are top risks identified for the selected option.
- Is there a fallback path if selected option fails.

## Communication Checks
- Can a reviewer understand why the selected option won.
- Are tradeoffs explained in plain language.
- Are unresolved issues visible and owned.
